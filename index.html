Andrea Lattuada (andrea.lattuada@inf.ethz.ch)

I'm a PhD Student in the Systems Group, Department of Computer Science at ETH Zürich. My advisor is Prof. Timothy Roscoe.

I have been supported in part by a Google PhD Fellowship.

My interests span various areas of systems, programming methodology, and software verification. I am currently focusing on fault-tolerance in streaming systems, language-supported enforcement of complex programming contracts, and tools and techniques for practical verification of large-scale systems software.
